# ⚜️ THE OBSIDIAN PALACE: MASTER ADMIN GUIDE ⚜️
*Official Operational Manual for the DINA COSMETIC Commerce Engine*

Welcome, Commander. This guide is designed to empower you with full control over your luxury e-commerce empire. From managing the "Product Vault" to fulfilling "The Manifest", every tool in your arsenal is detailed here.

---

## 🏛️ SECTION 1: THE ROYAL OVERVIEW (DASHBOARD)
The Dashboard is your strategic heart. It provides real-time intelligence on the health of your kingdom.

- **Gross Revenue**: All-time verified sales volume.
- **Total Orders**: Total volume of customer transactions.
- **Product Vault**: Current count of unique assets live on your storefront.
- **Low Inventory**: A critical alert system for assets requiring replenishment.
- **Recent Transactions**: Your live feed of incoming commerce.

**Pro-Tip**: Use the **"Sync Registry"** button if you need to force-refresh the data from the deep core.

---

## 💎 SECTION 2: THE PRODUCT VAULT (MANAGEMENT)
Your products are "Assets". Managing them requires precision.

### 1. Creating a New Asset
- Navigate to **Products**.
- Click **"Forge New Product"**.
- **Status**: Set to `Draft` while building; set to `Active` to push it live to the storefront instantly.
- **Pricing**: All pricing is handled server-side for security.

### 2. Managing Permutations (Variants)
Luxury often comes in shades, sizes, or volumes.
- Add **Variants** to any product.
- Each variant has its own **Dedicated Stock**.
- Ensure each variant has a clear name (e.g., "Obsidian Black", "50ml") for the client to see.

### 3. Inventory Control
- Monitor the **"Critical Replenishment"** widget on the Dashboard.
- When stock hits zero, the storefront will automatically mark the asset as **Sold Out** to prevent over-selling.

---

## 📜 SECTION 3: THE MANIFEST (ORDERS & FULFILLMENT)
Every order is an entry in the Manifest. Integrity is paramount.

### 1. Order Statuses
- **Pending**: Order initiated but payment not yet verified.
- **Paid**: The gold standard. Payment confirmed via Stripe.
- **Shipped**: Asset is in transit.
- **Cancelled/Refunded**: Operational reversals.

### 2. The Fulfillment Ritual
- Locate a **Paid** order in the list.
- Click **"Fulfill"**.
- This action automatically communicates with **Shippo** to calculate rates and prepare the dispatch.
- **Shipping Label**: Once fulfilled, click the **"Portal"** link to download and print your shipping label.
- **Client Notification**: The system will automatically send an elegant dispatch email via **Resend** once the status changes to Shipped.

---

## 🎨 SECTION 4: THE OBSIDIAN CMS (DIGITAL STORYTELLING)
The "Experiences" section allows you to architect your storefront without code.

### 1. Forging a New Space
- Navigate to **Experiences**.
- Click **"Forge New Space"** to create a page with a custom URL (Slug).
- **Architecting**: Click the "Architect" button on any page to enter the creative canvas.

### 2. The Component Vault
Inside the architect's canvas, you can drag and drop these specialized assets:
- **Hero Banner**: Your high-impact visual first impression.
- **Product Gallery**: Showcase specific collections (e.g., "Trending Essentials").
- **Philosophy Block**: Share the brand's narrative and storytelling.
- **Visual Vibe**: Striking image-based banners.

### 3. Finalizing Digital Architecture
- Always click **"Finalize Digital Architecture"** to save your changes.
- **Publishing**: Use the **Globe** icon to push the experience live to your clientele. Use the **Lock** icon to take it offline.

---

## 📊 SECTION 5: ANALYTICS & INTELLIGENCE
Data is the currency of the modern empire.

- **Sales Overview**: Monitor the 7-day and 30-day revenue trends with high-precision line charts.
- **Average Order Value (AOV)**: Track the average spend per customer to measure the effectiveness of your collections.
- **Top Selling Products**: Identified by Roman numerals (I, II, III...), see which assets are dominating the market share.

---

## 🔐 SECTION 6: THE INVENTORY VAULT (HIGH-DENSITY LEDGER)
For auditors and stock commanders, the Vault provides a master record of every variant.

- **Central Ledger**: View every SKU, stock level, and price override in one list.
- **Security Levels**:
    - **SECURE**: Plenty of stock available.
    - **CRITICAL**: Stock is below 5 units. Replenish immediately.
    - **DEPLETED**: Stock is at zero.

## 🛡️ SECTION 7: COMMANDERS & PROTOCOLS (USERS)
- **User Roles**: Only "Admin" roles can access this portal. 
- **Customer Profiles**: View purchase history and contact details for your elite clientele.

## ⚙️ SECTION 8: GLOBAL CONFIGURATIONS
Navigate to **Settings** to control the core parameters:
- **Shipping Thresholds**: Adjust the "Free Shipping" minimum.
- **Store Status**: Temporary closures or maintenance modes.

---

## 🏆 SECTION 9: STEWARDSHIP BEST PRACTICES
To maintain the prestige of the DINA COSMETIC brand:

1. **High-Resolution Visuals**: Always use professional, high-definition images for the "Product Vault" and "Experiences".
2. **Atomic Updates**: When editing product variants, click "Save" immediately to ensure stock synchronization.
3. **Swift Fulfillment**: Aim to fulfill orders within 24 hours of the "Paid" status to maintain client trust.
4. **Content Archiving**: Instead of deleting pages, use the "Draft" status to archive seasonal experiences.

---

## 🆘 SUPPORT & TROUBLESHOOTING
- **Orders stuck in 'Pending'**: This usually means the client didn't complete the Stripe checkout.
- **Label Errors**: Ensure the customer provided a valid physical address before attempting fulfillment.
- **Sync Issues**: Use the "Sync Registry" button on the Dashboard for a force-refresh.
